FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a lawn care service cost? +
Pricing depends on property size, service type, and frequency. Most residential mowing starts around $45–$85 per visit. We provide free, detailed estimates — no guesswork, no hidden fees.
Do I need to be home during the service? +
Nope! As long as we have access to the yard, we can complete your service whether you're home or away. We'll send a completion notification when we're done.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Absolutely. Verdant Landscape Co. is fully licensed and insured. We carry general liability and worker's compensation insurance on every job. Proof of insurance is available on request.
Do you offer contracts or one-time services? +
Both! We offer flexible recurring service plans (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly) and one-time services for projects, seasonal cleanups, and special occasions. No long-term contracts required.
