Our Story

15 years of craft, community, and an unwavering commitment to outdoor spaces that feel like home.

How It Started

Built on a
Love for the Land

Verdant Landscape Co. was founded in 2009 by a team of landscape professionals who believed that every property — no matter the size — deserved to look exceptional. What started as a two-truck operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted landscaping companies.

We've maintained that founder mentality: showing up on time, communicating clearly, and doing work we're proud to put our name on. Today we serve hundreds of residential and commercial clients across the area, backed by a crew of dedicated landscape professionals who genuinely love what they do.
